Overview

Vehicle Route Tracking System is a system Software used by transport and fleet companies to monitor the location of cars, buses, trucks, trailers and other vehicles. The software is connected to the vehicles via GPS, giving Drivers and companies real-time insight into the geographic position of their vehicles
